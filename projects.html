Spirits of Liberty: The Contradictions of an Intoxicating Inheritance

A comprehensive historical analysis tracing the complex, multifaceted roles of alcohol across American history—from colonial conquest and the transatlantic slave trade through the temperance movement and national Prohibition. Winner of the Harold W. Miller Prize for best honors thesis.

Honors Thesis
